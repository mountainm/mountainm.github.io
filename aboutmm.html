About Mountain Mission

At Mountain Mission we believe that everyone can and should enjoy nature to the fullest and that there a level and type of adventure exists for everyone. We specialise in assisting our clients to discover and undertake outdoor adventures, mainly in mountaineous terrain and with a focus on the Scottish Highlands, although other areas are also possible.

We also recognise the unique opportunities that outdoor adventures can offer and ways that they can help ones wellbeing. This includes physical and mental challenges and achievements, but also spiritual wellbeing. Being out, away from technology in the beauty of nature with very few daily distractions, allows for reflection on some of lifes deeper questions. We are open to assisting in these processes.

We are also a Christian organisation and aim to maintain these norms and values to the fullest in the way we operate. We also offer Christian specific trips for those seeking deeper spiritual well-being.

Mountain Mission came into existance in 2017 and is run by Simeon Calvert. Simeon has many years of experience in hiking throughout Europe and the UK and has a great love for all the mountains and hills have to offer. He is a trained UK Mountain Leader, and member of Mountaineering Scotland and Dutch mountaineering association NKBV. Originally born and raised in Scotland, he now lives in the Netherlands, from where he also offers unique opportunities for Dutch based clients to enjoy the Highlands with a Scottish guide who speaks their own Dutch language.
